Further reading

Check out these other websites if you are looking for more Puyo Puyo content!

Puyo Nexus: home to a very comprehensive fan wiki with in-depth information on the history of every game, trivia, and random obscure lore. Also includes useful gameplay guides, such as the stairs setup tutorial that was mentioned on the How to Play page.

Puyo trainer: practice building chains at the pace you want. You can stop time and move puyos backwards! Can be played on a mobile device easily.
Or, you can try Puyo Nexus' chain simulator to build your own chains from scratch.

If you are interested in getting started with playing a game, try Puyo Puyo Tetris 2, the most recent English-localized installation of the series! I would recommend the earliest games, but you can't really obtain those legally in English.
PC: Steam
Nintendo Switch: Nintendo store
