Welcome to Puyo Puyo!


Puyo Puyo is a video game franchise that had its first game released in 1991 by now-dissolved Japanese developement company Compile. It revolves around a falling-block style puzzle game in which the player has to match four slime-like creatures (called Puyo) of the same color to make them disappear. The creator believed other puzzle games of the time had "weak" characters, so the Puyo Puyo series features a colorful cast of characters originally from Compile's Madou Monogatari RPG series and has a much larger focus on its characters compared to many other puzzle games. Puyo Puyo is quite popular in Japan, but it has sadly not taken off in western countries. There are many games that have not been localized into English, but I hope that if their popularity grows they will be released to the world soon. I made this website to introduce Puyo Puyo to my friends and help new fans learn more about the series. Feel free to visit any of the pages below for more information!
